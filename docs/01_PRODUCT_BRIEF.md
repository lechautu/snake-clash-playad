# Product Brief

## Title
Snake Clash Arena – Playable Ad Prototype

## Goal
Build a lightweight, readable, and satisfying top-down snake arena prototype for mobile playable ad presentation.

## Player Experience
The player enters a compact arena full of food and rival AI snakes. By steering with a virtual joystick, the player grows longer and stronger, defeats or cuts rival snakes, triggers milestone chest spawns, and gathers coins from chest bursts. The match is won by collecting 200 coins or lost on death.

## Core Pillars
- Immediate control readability
- Visible growth and progression
- Short-session tension from rival snakes
- Reward spikes through chest and coin bursts
- Simple but satisfying collision outcomes

## Why This Works for a Playable Ad
- The game looks active immediately
- The player understands the loop quickly: move, eat, grow, avoid danger, get coins
- The coin bar provides a clear short-term objective
- Rival snakes create tension and highlight moments without needing deep systems
- The visual result of cutting or killing a snake is rewarding and easy to read

## MVP Scope
- Fixed arena
- Player snake
- Several AI snakes
- Food growth loop
- Level-based head-to-head rule
- Head-to-body cut rule
- Chest milestone system
- Coin burst and coin bar
- Win/fail states
- Retry

## Non-Goals
- Full .io production game
- Online multiplayer
- Meta economy
- Deep AI behavior
- Multiple levels or content scalability

## Presentation Target
The prototype should feel polished enough to be shown in browser-based environments such as Luna Playground.
